Methodology
Step 1: Data Collection
Collect images and videos of Indian Sign Language users showing non-manual features such as facial expressions, eye movements, and head movements.
Step 2: Data Preprocessing
Clean and organize the collected data. Remove unclear or duplicate samples.
Step 3: Feature Extraction
Use MediaPipe and OpenCV to detect facial landmarks, eye movements, and head positions.
Step 4: Feature Analysis
Analyze the extracted non-manual features and identify their meaning in sign language communication.
Step 5: Text Conversion
Convert the identified features into meaningful text output.
Step 6: Testing and Evaluation
Test the system using sample videos and evaluate the accuracy of text generation.# Methodology
